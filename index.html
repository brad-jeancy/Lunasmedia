Got questions?

Here are the most common things clients ask before booking. If you need to know anything else, just reach out directly.

What is your pricing? +

Pricing is project-based and tailored to your specific needs. After our initial consultation, I provide a detailed quote covering all aspects of the shoot.

How far in advance should I book? +

I recommend booking 4-6 weeks in advance for personal portraits and 8-12 weeks for commercial or editorial projects to ensure availability.

Do you travel for shoots? +

Yes, I frequently travel for assignments. Travel costs are outlined transparently in the project proposal.
